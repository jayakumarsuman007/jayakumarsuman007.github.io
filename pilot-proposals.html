Pilot Proposals

Below are example pilot outlines designed for Tier 2/3 cities like Gulbarga. These are templates you can customise and share with CSR partners or clinics.

1) 3-month RPM Pilot — 50 patients

  • Objective: Test vitals monitoring workflow and referral thresholds
  • Devices: Pulse oximeter, BP cuff, thermometer
  • Deliverables: Dashboard access, weekly reports, handover pack

2) Community Screening + Imaging Assist

  • Objective: Screen 500 patients and triage via AI-assisted chest X-ray enhancement
